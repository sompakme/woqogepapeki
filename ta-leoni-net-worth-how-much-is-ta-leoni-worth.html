Personal Life

Téa Leoni’s personal life has had its fair share of ups and downs, characterized by marriages, divorces, and new beginnings.

In 1991, Leoni married Neil Joseph Tardio, Jr., a television commercial producer. However, their marriage ended in divorce in 1995.

Two years later, she found love again and tied the knot with actor David Duchovny. The couple welcomed two children during their marriage, a daughter named Madelaine and a son named Kyd. Unfortunately, their relationship faced challenges, and they separated in 2008. Their divorce was finalized in 2014.

Despite the turmoil, Leoni found love once more in her co-star from the television series “Madam Secretary,” Tim Daly. The couple began dating in 2014, showcasing a blossoming relationship in both their personal and professional lives.

Téa Leoni’s personal life has been a journey of love, loss, and resilience. Through it all, she has remained dedicated to her career and to finding happiness.
