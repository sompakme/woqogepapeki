The Cast Of 'Ladies Of London' Ranked From Richest To Poorest

Ladies of London is an American reality TV show that aired for one season in 2014. The hook that grabbed audience’s attention was not just the beautiful English accents, but also that the women in front of the camera were accomplished and driven. Some of these gals were born in the United Kingdom, while others moved there during different stages of life. What they all have in common is their love of luxury, socialite status, and the shared bond between immense cultural differences from America to Great Britain.

As previously mentioned, many of these ladies have put in massive amounts of hard work to gain their status. All of their blood, sweat, and tears must have paid off, because these women are currently living very comfortable lifestyles. Read further to find out which lady of London is the richest and who’s the poorest.

10 Adela King Is Worth About $6 Million

Adela King has been all over the world. She was born in Costa Rica, grew up in Florida, and was introduced to England when she was signed up to attend boarding school in the country. As a fashionista, she has raised her net worth to $6 million by starting her own line of boots, running a maternity clothing business, and working as a model.

9 Juliet Angus Has An Estimated $6 Million Net Worth

Not only a Lady of London,Juliet Angus is also an image consultant. She was born in Chicago before joining her reality costars and had established a name for herself prior to the show. Juliet is a celebrity stylist, fashion blogger, and is involved in “high-society” life regarding almost anything fashion. Her image has boosted her net worth to about $6 million.

8 Julie Montagu Currently Has $10 Million

Julie Montagu, Viscountess Hinchingbrooke is our first Ladies of London star to break a double-digit net worth with $10 million. She is a woman of many talents and has immersed herself into the life of a yoga instructor, a blogger, a writer (Superfoods, Recharge: A Year of Self-Care to Focus on You, and others), an entrepreneur, and last but not least, an American TV personality on reality television.

7 Sophie Stanbury Has A $15 Million Net Worth

Sophie Stanbury is a United Kingdom native and has history with one of our other London Ladies. She was previously married to Alex, Caroline Stanbury’s brother, which is why these two women share a last name. Sophie amassed her $15 million net worth by starting her own design company and involving herself in the high life of London.

6 Marissa Hermer's Net Worth Is At $20 Million

Marissa Hermer was born and grew up along the western coast of America before moving to London in her 20’s. She got a public relations career in motion, then decided to move back to the states, but this time chose to reside in New York. Marissa worked in luxury fashion, luxury dining, and nightclubs thanks to her involvement with PR and media. She has opened her own solo restaurant as well as published a cookbook, leading her net worth to spike to $20 million.

5 Noelle Reno Is Worth $20 Million

Noelle Reno was born in America but moved to England to further pursue her dreams. She has dipped her toe into the water of several high-end jobs, including modeling, being a television presenter, a fashion blogger, and currently a London socialite. At only 37, these experiences have raised her current net worth to $20 million.

4 Annabelle Neilson Had Amassed $20 Million

Annabelle Neilson, a previous Ladies of London star, was unfortunately found dead in July of 2018. Prior to her passing, she had gained a net worth of $20 million. She was most famously known for her involvement as a model (best friends with Kate Moss), and as a muse for the great fashion designer Alexander McQueen.

3 Caroline Stanbury Is Worth About $30 Million

Caroline Stanbury is the definition of high society. With a net worth of about $30 million, she constantly bumps elbows with elites and socialites. Prior to joining the Ladies of London cast, she was a top personal stylist. She had married (and divorced almost two decades later) a millionaire Turkish investment banker, which is where majority of her wealth comes from.

2 Caprice Bourret Has A $30 Million Net Worth

Caprice Bourret is an American businesswoman who currently resides in London. She is a woman of many talents; while she currently runs her company (By Caprice), she has also dabbled in modeling, acting, and working as a television personality… obviously. All of this work has led the Ladies of London star to hold a net worth of $30 million.

1 Caroline Fleming Currently Has $450 Million

Unlike the rest of the ladies, who were born in either America or England, Caroline Fleming is a native of Denmark. She has been busy for most of the 46 years of her life, pushing herself to become a Danish noble entrepreneur, start modeling, and even own Valdemar’s Castle for eight years. Caroline has written two books, and all of this success has given her an astounding $450 million net worth.
