IREC 2024 is an international conference dedicated to the advancement of renewable energy and conservation. This highly anticipated event serves as a platform for experts, researchers, and industry professionals to exchange ideas and explore the latest developments in the field. With a focus on renewable energy grid infrastructures, technologies, engineering design methods, and industrial challenges, IREC 2024 aims to drive innovation and propel the industry forward.

Publication & Indexing

All papers submitted to IREC 2024, the international conference on renewable energy and conservation, will undergo a rigorous peer-review process. Accepted papers will be published in the IREC conference proceedings, providing an opportunity for researchers and industry professionals to share their findings and contribute to the field of renewable energy.

The IREC conference proceedings hold significant value as they are widely recognized and indexed by renowned databases. This ensures enhanced visibility and accessibility of the published papers to a global audience of researchers and practitioners. The conference proceedings will be submitted for inclusion in databases such as Ei Compendex and Scopus, further increasing their reach and impact.

In addition to being published in the conference proceedings, selected extended versions of papers registered and presented at IREC 2024 will be encouraged to submit to the highly respected “International Journal of Green Energy.” This prestigious journal is indexed by SCIE and Scopus, ensuring that the research presented at IREC receives maximum recognition and dissemination within the scientific community.

Benefits of Publication & Indexing

By having their work published in the IREC conference proceedings and the International Journal of Green Energy, authors gain several key benefits:

  • Increased visibility and recognition within the renewable energy research community
  • Opportunity to contribute to the scientific knowledge base and advance the field
  • Networking opportunities with fellow researchers and industry professionals
  • Chance to receive valuable feedback and insights from peers in the field
  • Possibility of future collaborations and joint research ventures

Overall, publication and indexing in reputable platforms like the IREC conference proceedings and the International Journal of Green Energy provide researchers with a vital platform to showcase their work, exchange ideas, and significantly contribute to the advancement of renewable energy technologies and practices.
