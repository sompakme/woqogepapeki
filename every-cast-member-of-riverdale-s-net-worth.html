Filled with drama, mystery, and some comedy, the series follows the murder of Jason Blossom and those who try to figure out the mystery that is his death. Lili Reinhart - $2 million

Lili Reinhart, an American actress, developed a love for singing, dancing, and acting at a very young age. She even left her home at the young age of 18 to pursue her dream of becoming an actress. Her career began in 2011 and her acting has only gotten better, as she begins to take on larger and larger roles. In the past, she has been in productions such as Lilith (2011), Not Waving But Drowning (2012), and The Good Neighbor (2016). At the young age of just 22 years old, she has made quite a name for herself and continues to make her presence more and more noticed in Hollywood.

Marisol Nichols - $3 million

Marisol Nichols is a talented American actress who has quite an extensive filmography profile compared to some of the people on this list. She has been seen in Scream 2 (1997), The Princess and the Marine (2001), Felon (2008) and is also cast to play Angie Garza in The Organ Donar. In Riverdale, though, she plays the mother of Veronica Lodge: Hermione Lodge. She is so important in the story that she gets her own storyline as she faces the mess that her husband Hiram Lodge left for her before going to jail. Hermione is a strong-willed and head-strong character, who seems to always make it through.

Camila Mendes - $3 million

Camila Mendes is the young lady who plays the elegant and, at first, snooty Veronica Lodge. She and her mother (above) had just moved to Riverdale from New York, and Veronica wasn't very happy about it. Camila brings this role to life, being able to play the brave, young teen to gracefully as she stands up to anything that gets in the way of her and her friends. You can see Camila in The New Romantic, The Perfect Date, and Coyote Lake, even though Riverdale was her first major project in 2017. Talk about a lot of pressure. She handles it well, though!

KJ Apa - $3 million

KJ Apa is the star of the show, playing the man of the hour: Archie Andrews. Now, the show doesn't seem to have one main character, even though the comics did; it seems to be a show surrounding a group of people and their individual problems. So while Archie is not the main character, he still is very important. KJ Apa is, in fact, a bright-eyed actor from New Zealand who moved to Hollywood at a young age, like many of his co-stars. You can see him in other productions such as A Dog's Purpose, The Hate U Give, and The Last Summer. He has also been nominated for five awards, winning one of them!

Cole Sprouse - $9 million

Cole Sprouse is known for a lot of things, one of them being his major role as Cody in The Suite Life of Zack and Cody, The Suite Life on Deck, and The Suite Life Movie. After finally finishing the franchise in his late teens, he decided to take a break from acting, disappearing from the acting world from 2012 to 2017, where he shows up in Riverdale as Jughead Jones. He plays a mysterious young man who has had a hard life. Fun fact: Cole Sprouse and his co-star, Camila Mendes, actually graduated at the same University, during the same year.
