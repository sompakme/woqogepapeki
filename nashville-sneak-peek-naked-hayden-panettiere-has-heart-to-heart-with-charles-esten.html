Nashville Sneak Peek: Naked Hayden Panettiere Has Heart to Heart With Charles Esten

Juliette Barnes will stop at nothing to convince Deacon Clayborne to join her on tour.

In a sneak preview from the Wednesday, Oct. 24 episode of ABC’s Nasvhille, Juliette (Hayden Panettiere, 23) lounges naked in bed with Deacon (Charles Esten, 47) while discussing their future together. The only person standing in their way? Country legend Rayna James (Connie Britton, 45).

“The other night, why’d you call Rayna up instead of me?” Juliette asks.

Deacon sighs and responds, “I didn’t do it to hurt you.”

“It didn’t hurt me,” Juliette counters. “Just, sometimes, I’d like to be that girl worth choosing.”
