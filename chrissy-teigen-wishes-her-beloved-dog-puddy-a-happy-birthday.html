Chrissy Teigen Wishes Her Beloved Dog Puddy a Happy Birthday

Dogs are family too! Chrissy Teigen wished her beloved dog Puddy a happy birthday on Instagram on Thursday, March 17, and she’s not ashamed to be “those people.”

“Before the chaos of two more dogs and a baby, there was only you, our Puddy!! My boy. I love you and your graying paws and chops to the moon and back. Happy 8th birthday! Yes, we are those people 😩❤️😩❤️,” the pregnant Sports Illustrated model, 30, captioned a photo of herself with Puddy and husband John Legend.

The couple are also parents to dog Pippa and three-legged bulldog Penny.

In October, the Cravings cookbook author and musician announced they are expecting their first child together.
