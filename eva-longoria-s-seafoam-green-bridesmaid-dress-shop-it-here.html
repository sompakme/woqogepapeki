Serving as a bridesmaid in her friend's wedding, Eva Longoria was snapped running through the streets of Cordoba, Spain, on Friday, May 1, with a big smile on her face—and no shoes on her feet!

Just like the other attendants, the Desperate Housewives alum, 40, rocked a pastel blue-green chiffon dress with a sweetheart neckline, cinched at the waist with a bedazzled belt. She held a small bouquet in hand and hiked her dress up above her knees to navigate the city's cobblestone streets.
