Ryan Briscoe Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Ryan Briscoe (born 24 September 1981) is an Australian auto racing driver from Sydney who has competed in open wheel and sports car competitions across Europe and America. He is the son of former Australian cricketer Ryan Briscoe. IndyCar Series driver for Chip Ganassi Racing, he is now in his second season. Briscoe is fluent in English, as well as Italian and French, in addition to his native tongue. He went to Trinity Grammar School in Summer Hill, Sydney, Australia, where he graduated with honours. He is married to Nicole Briscoe (née Manske), an ESPN motorsports reporter who he met in college and married in 2009.
