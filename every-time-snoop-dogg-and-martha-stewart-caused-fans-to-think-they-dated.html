Every Time Snoop Dogg And Martha Stewart Caused Fans To Think They Dated

Snoop Dogg and Martha Stewart share one of the most wholesome, adorably unlikely friendships of showbusiness.

The rapper and the homemaker and businesswoman have puzzled many fans with their seemingly unshakeable bond, one that goes way back to 2008, when Snoop first appeared on Stewart's show, 'The Martha Stewart Show'. Nearly 15 years later, the BFF duo is still going strong, prompting some to wonder whether their relationship has ever turned romantic, or ever will.

The answer is no: Stewart and Dogg have never dated. The rapper has been married to his teenage sweetheart Shante Broadus since 1997, while Martha Stewart married publisher Andrew Stewart in 1961, before ultimately getting divorced in 1990. She has never remarried.

Despite being just pals, there have been some instances that led to speculations about a possible romance, confusing some fans who would like for their relationship to be more than just a friendship. Let's have a look.

8 Snoop Dogg And Martha Stewart Proved They Have Amazing Chemistry

In 2008, Snoop Dogg appeared on 'The Martha Stewart Show,' where they made mashed potatoes together.

In between some spud squishing and mixing, the duo shared a nice conversation and gave proof of their great chemistry and banter in front ot the cameras as Stewart asked questions about Dogg's slang.

Enough to assume something more was going on behind the scenes? For some, maybe, but the relationship seemed to be purely platonic.

Snoop, at this point almost a star of daytime television thanks to Stewart, went back to her show in 2009, when the duo made brownies, with the rapper alluding to adding marijuana and Stewart playing along.

7 Martha Stewart Said She Wanted To See More Of Snoop Dogg

After Snoop's two guest appearances, his relationship with Martha was on hold for a while. In 2014, Stewart revealed in a Q&A session on Reddit that she would love to be better friends with the rapper.

"I wish I were closer friends with Snoop Dogg," the host replied to a fan asking about their bond.

Needless to say, many of the questions she received on Reddit were about her relationship to Snoop Dogg, proving that some people were curious about it and not at all convinced that nothing romantic was going on.

"I've never tasted Snoop's cookies, so I don't know," Stewart also said when a person asked if she's ever tasted the rapper's special cookies, alluding to marijuana.

At the end of the session, Stewart updated her thread on Reddit with a direct message to Snoop.

"Where's Snoop? I have brownies FOR YOU!" she wrote.

6 Martha Stewart And Snoop Dogg Reunited To Roast Justin Bieber And Sparks Flew

It wasn't long before the pair reunited on screen. In 2015, Martha and Snoop were back together on 'The Roast of Justin Bieber' on Comedy Central.

The two of them sat next to each another on stage and laughed at their respective jokes and roasts, proving once again how much fun they have together. Years later, Snoop revealed Martha's sass displayed on the night was the reason he wanted to spend as much time as possible with her.

"I knew I wanted to be alongside this lady for the rest of my life," the rapper said in 2019 (around the 1:25 minute mark in the video above).

5 Snoop Has Said Some Really Nice Things About Martha

Snoop Dogg has always had nothing but kind, genuine words about Stewart. In an interview with 'Rolling Stone' in 2016, the rapper was filming its first show with the homemaker, 'Martha and Snoop’s Dinner Party' on VH1, in which they hosted various celebrity guests and went back to where it all started years prior: a shiny kitchen.

"I've never met anyone like Martha Stewart," Dogg said of Martha at the time.

"When we come together, it’s a natural combination of love, peace, and harmony," he continued.

4 Martha And Snoop Shot A Romantic, 'Ghost' Inspired Promo For Their Show

When promoting 'Martha and Snoop’s Dinner Party', the two celebrities went all the way and played with how the public perceived them as a maybe couple.

The promo for their cooking show is inspired by the romantic tearjerker movie 'Ghost,' with Martha and Snoop recreating the ultra-famous pottery scene where Demi Moore's character is molding a vase together with the ghost of her deceased boyfriend, played by Patrick Swayze.

Except here, Martha unbuttons her collared shirt and starts frosting a chocolate cake as it spins on a platter. That's when Snoop comes in, sporting a white tank top and a bandana, and sits behind her as they continue frosting together. Martha even lets Snoop lick frosting off her fingers!

3 Martha And Snoop Shot *Another* Romantic Promo As Jack And Rose From 'Titanic'

In 2019, Snoop and Martha were gearing up for the season three premiere of their 'Potluck Dinner Party' series.

To amp up the hype for their cooking show, the two hosts and friends fully embraced the romance rumors and reenacted the iconic 'Titanic' scene where Jack and Rose are on the bow of the ship.

In the clip, Martha stars as Jack and Snoop as Rose, while potatoes play once again a crucial role. Not mashed this time around, but fried, as Snoop declared in a spoof line of the original scene: "I'm frying," he says as he holds two french fry baskets in hand — and the result is hilarious.

2 Martha Wrote The Foreword Of Snoop's First Cookbook

Martha has always been supportive of Snoop, having nothing but praise to spare when the rapper released his first-ever cookbook in 2018 (of which she wrote the foreword, of course). The title? 'From Crook to Cook: Platinum Recipes from Tha Boss Dogg's Kitchen.' Catchy.

"I actually like to watch him cook," Stewart admitted on the "Today" show in 2019.

"He's so particular ... it all finally comes together," she explained.

1 Martha's Aware That Everyone Is Curious About Their Relationship

In 2020, Martha told 'Us Food' that "everybody's" trying to figure her and Snoop out, clearly aware of the interest in the details about their relationship.

"I think it's just that we're an odd pair and people like contrast, right?" she said.

As of 2022, the BBFs continue going strong, with Snoop making a surprise appearance in Stewart's commercial for her 19 Crimes Martha's Chard, calling her "baby girl" and praising her wine.

With a friendship like this (sorry, Martha and Snoop shippers!), it is safe to say we'll get to see more of this dynamic duo soon.
