One might always feel extremely fantastic when he or she use the extra money on a blow out or a manicure. Apart from that one should also feel the best treatments that are offered at the best spas in the world. The services at these different locations range from herb infused massages, dust facials and treatments of the scalp and much more. The following are the 10 most expensive spa treatments in the world that spas can offer for the good health and enjoyment.

10. Aroma-Listic Day Spa pedicure

It is a treatment available at Agoura Hills, CA spa. It is considered a very special pedicure and it costs $200. The argument is that it slathers your feet in the mixture of white chocolate strawberry truffle in the course of the treatment process.

9. Good Luck Ritual

The most likable service that is offered by the spa at Encore Wynn, in Las Vegas. It is a treatment that takes approximately 80 minutes and it applies the principles of Feng Shui in harmonizing the body. It begins with a custom massage of up to 50 minutes with Thai oils and herbs and later to a botanical scalp treatment of wild lime. This costs $250 for a foot and hand treatment.

8. Elysian Cleanse

It is also called classic spa treatment that is available in Chicago Elysian Hotel in downtown. The cleanse costs $300. The treatment consists of water therapy, the calming cocoon that exfoliates and then renews the client's skin which sometimes is referred to as a Seaweed gel wrap and the marine massage.

7. Caviar conditioner

This is the treatment that has been used by such rich and wealth people like Catherine Zeta-Jones at South Kensington Spa which cost $400 to have a shampoo treatment. This price was in 2007 and it is subjected to have increased with the passing of time. It is believed that Jones had Beluga caviar flown from Iran to give her hair color as well as a richer texture. The treatment also consists of a truffle based shampoo.

6. UMO gold facial

UMO is an American famous facial that uses twenty four karat gold. The gold is used because it has high hydration advantages and also adds nutrients to the skin. It costs $400.

5. Royal Treatment

It is found in China at Shanghai's Bund Five Spa Oasis. Getting this type of treatment at this place is very essential. The treatment involves soaking in a bath of oils and rose petals and then gets a body scrub of red wine, another four hands massage, 90 minute Pramol facial and lavender-body scrub. It costs $451.

4. Microablation and Triphasic Combination Facial

These spa services are available at the Cornelia Day Spa in the city of New York. The treatment is popular in prominent people and celebrities like Gwyneth Paltrow and Kate Moss that have ever had the taste of this premium treatment. The electromagnetism is used to simulate the collagen, heat and also vibration to sculpt the face of the client and can be compared to having plastic surgery without going under the knife. The treatment costs $500 dollars.

3. Indulgences

The treatment costs approximately U.S. $666 and it is considered a very special promotion bringing a question of whether it is really a promotion. The treatment is offered at the Mandarin Oriental Spa in Japan's city Tokyo. It was created to honor the 5th anniversary of the Spa. It includes the following Oriental Essence treatment of the body, foot ritual aromatherapy facial and also a fruit platter that is seasonal.

2. Grand Luxe Facial

This facial is offered at spa Radiance which is found in San Francisco and the treatment costs $750. It includes a caviar mask , diamond peel, omega 6 treatment meant for the eyes, protein drops for the hands, LED light therapy that clears out the rosacea, black heads, red spots, and also both a hand massage and foot massage

1. Evian bath

This is believed to be the most expensive bath in the whole world. It is offered by SoBe's Hotel Victor to their guests. Those who wish to indulge in this bath will lounge in a 1000 liters of an Evian water filled tub with fuchsia Gerber daises. Among the first people to try out this bath at $5000 was Serena Williams. The treatment offers one a chance to snack smoked salmon lollipops, chocolate and foie gras while relaxing in the waters.
