All-Atlantic Title Match, Tony Khan Announcement, And More Set For 2/22 AEW Dynamite

Five matches are set for next week’s .

As AEW continues the build to Revolution on March 5, several matches are taking shape for the February 22 Dynamite. It was previously announced that a Revolution Tag Team Battle Royale will take place with several teams competing for a shot to be added the four-way title match at Revolution. We now know that Aussie Open, Top Flight, and RUSH and Preston Vance will enter that battle royale.

After a confrontation on Rampage, we also know that Orange Cassidy will defend his All-Atlantic Championship against “former friend” Wheeler Yuta. Yuta was once aligned with Best Friends before joining Blackpool Combat Club. Likewise, BCC’s Jon Moxley will take on Evil Uno in singles competition.

The Acclaimed will take on Big Bill and Lee Moriarty, plus Saraya will be in action against Skye Blue. Additionally, Christian Cage will speak to Tony Schiavone, and Tony Khan will make a “big” announcement.

Below is the updated lineup for February 22:

  • : Orange Cassidy (c) vs. Wheeler Yuta
  • Revolution Tag Team Battle Royale
  • Jon Moxley vs. Evil Uno
  • The Acclaimed vs. Big Bill & Lee Moriarty
  • Saraya vs. Skye Blue
  • Christian Cage speaks with Tony Schiavone
  • Tony Khan makes an announcement

WrestleZone will have complete coverage of the show as it airs on Wednesday.
