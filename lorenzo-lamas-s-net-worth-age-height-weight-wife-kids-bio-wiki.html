Early Life

The American actor was born on January 20, 1957, in Santa Monica, California, to his actor parents. When he was a little kid, his parents got separated.

Thereafter, he moved to New York City. There is not much information about his early education, but he is said to have graduated from ‘Admiral Farragut Academy’ in 1975. Following that, he took the martial art training.
