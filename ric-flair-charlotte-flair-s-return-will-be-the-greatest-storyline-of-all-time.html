Ric Flair: Charlotte Flairs Return Will Be The Greatest Storyline Of All Time

Ric Flair is confident that Charlotte Flair‘s return will be special.

Charlotte Flair went down with an injury on the December 8 episode of WWE SmackDown. WWE announced that she will be out of action for nine months. “The Queen” is reportedly set to have surgery on a torn ACL, MCL, and meniscus.

Speaking with Justin Dhillon on Casual Conversations with The Classic, Ric Flair discussed Charlotte Flair’s injury.

“She is the best worker in the business, male or female. She just gets better every day. I told her, she’ll come back from this injury. It stresses her out because she hasn’t been hurt in a long time. But she’ll come through the surgery, and she’ll come back. It’ll be the greatest storyline of all time. She and Bayley and IYO and all those guys, and Asuka.

“There are more people for her to still wrestle. She’s got so much going on for her. I’m not knocking any of the other girls at all, but someone’s gotta be the best. That’s just the way it is.”
