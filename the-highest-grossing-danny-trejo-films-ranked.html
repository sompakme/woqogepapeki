The Highest-Grossing Danny Trejo Films Ranked

Born May 16, 1944, Danny Trejo is an American actor and entrepreneur. Trejo was born in Echo Park, Los Angeles, California. He is well-known for his harsh and menacing on-screen persona, and has appeared in over 200 film and television projects over his career. Trejo had a difficult childhood, being engaged with gangs and spending time in prison. After his release, he reformed his life and began an acting career. Trejo has appeared in several television shows in addition to his film career. Besides his acting, He is actively involved in charitable endeavors and works to inspire and mentor at-risk youth. He uses his own experiences to help others make positive changes in their lives.

Death Race 3: Inferno- $76 Million

Death Race 3: Inferno is a 2013 action film directed by Roel Reiné that serves as a follow-up to the 2008 film Death Race. The movie follows a group of convicts participating in a brutal racing competition for their freedom. Goldberg, played by Danny Trejo, is one of the seasoned racers in the Death Race. He is known for his great and aggressive driving style and ability to handle any challenges. Goldberg is a rough and scary figure who will go to any length to win the race and regain his freedom.

Muppets Most wanted- $80.4 Million

Danny Trejo makes a surprise appearance in this epic film as himself. Muppets Most Wanted is a 2014 musical comedy film directed by James Bobin and a sequel to the 2011 film The Muppets. The movie follows the Muppets as they embark on a world tour, unaware that they are being used by a criminal mastermind who resembles their leader, Kermit the Frog. The film was a commercial success grossing $80.4 million.

Once Upon a Time in Mexico- $98 Million

Once Upon a Time in Mexico film was released in 2003 and directed by Robert Rodriguez. Danny Trejo plays the character of Cucuy. It is the final installment in Rodriguez's Mexico Trilogy, preceded by El Mariachi and Desperado. Cucuy is a skilled hitman and one of the secondary antagonists in the film. He is a member of the corrupt Barillo cartel, working under the command of drug lord Armando Barillo (played by Willem Dafoe). Cucuy is known for his brutality and loyalty to Barillo, carrying out violent acts on his orders.

Anaconda- $136.8 Million

Anaconda is produced by Sony Pictures featuring Jennifer Lopez, Ice Cube, and Danny Trejo, among others. Trejo portrays Poacher, a documentary film crew member who ventures into the Amazon rain forest in search of a legendary giant anaconda. As the story unfolds, the crew encounters danger and conflict with the jungle's deadly creatures and other perils. Trejo's character, Poacher, adds a layer of tension and intensity to the film as he becomes entangled in a dangerous situation. With his tough-guy persona, Trejo brings his unique presence to the role, contributing to the overall suspense and action in the movie.

Spy Kids- $147 Million

This franchise film, created and directed by Robert Rodriguez, consists of several films released between 2001 and 2011. Danny Trejo plays the character of Isador (Machete) Cortez. Machete is a spy and member of the OSS (Organization of Super Spies), working alongside the main characters, Carmen and Juni Cortez, played by Alexa PenaVega and Daryl Sabara, respectively. He is known for his combat skills and expertise with various weapons. He is a mentor and ally to the young spies, providing guidance and assistance throughout their missions.

Predators- $160.5 Million

Predators, an action film directed by Nimród Antal, Danny Trejo plays the character of Cuchillo. It is a science fiction action film that is a sequel to the original 1987 Predator.Cuchillo, portrayed by Danny Trejo, is one of the groups of mercenaries who find themselves trapped on an alien planet and become the prey of the Predators, extraterrestrial hunters. Cuchillo is a Mexican drug cartel enforcer known for his ruthless nature and knife proficiency. He continues to show his tough-guy persona and brings his unique presence to the film.

Heat- $187 Million

Michael Mann directs Heat; Danny Trejo plays the character of Trejo, also known as Trejo's Gang Member. Heat is a crime thriller that features an ensemble cast, including Al Pacino, Robert De Niro, and Val Kilmer. Trejo's character is a member of Neil McCauley's (Robert De Niro) crew, which consists of skilled criminals specializing in high-stakes heists. Trejo portrays his character as known for his expertise in various criminal activities, including armed robberies. He is loyal to McCauley and participates in the group's criminal endeavors. This film was a great commercial success grossing $187 million.

Con Air- $224 Million

Con Air is an action film released in 1997 and directed by Simon West; Danny Trejo plays the character of Johnny Baca. Con Air follows the story of Cameron Poe, a former Army Ranger who finds himself aboard a prison transport plane filled with dangerous criminals. Johnny Baca, portrayed by Danny Trejo, is one of the convicts aboard the plane. He is a member of a group of prisoners led by Cyrus (The Virus) Grissom (played by John Malkovich). He is known for his ruthless nature, serving as one of the primary antagonists in the film.
