Timothee Chalamet May Be Hesitant To Get Serious With Kylie Jenner As They Push Back Against Break-U

Highlights

  • Kylie Jenner and Timothee Chalamet spotted on a dinner date, suggesting they are still together but facing challenges in their relationship.
  • Sources reveal that Timothee and Kylie are keeping things casual without putting a label on their relationship, mainly due to their busy schedules.
  • Reports suggest that Timothee struggles to keep up with Kylie's lifestyle and the negative attention it brings, prompting the couple to avoid the spotlight.

Kylie Jenner and Timothee Chalamet were recently spotted on a dinner date, shutting down the rumors that have been circulating for months suggesting the couple have gone separate ways. But while a new report says the couple remain together, it sounds like they have their challenges, as there’s been a dynamic shift in order to help salvage what’s left of their romance.

A source shared details about the celebrity couple’s relationship with PEOPLE Magazine, saying, “They’re both real with each other and things have been easy and fun.”

The recently enjoyed a double date night at New York City restaurant Tatiana, as per People Magazine. The source mentioned, “Timothee was really chill and so nice.” Another insider confirmed that the couple is still going strong but have become much more private.

Kylie And Timothee Reportedly Aren’t Committed

Perhaps even more telling, a source claimed to Us Weekly that they’re keeping things casual and not putting a label on it, despite being PDA-heavy last year when debuting their romance.

“Kylie and Timothée haven’t been spending that much time together recently because they both have very busy schedules,” they said. “But they’re still communicating on a pretty regular basis and have remained friends despite the fact that they don’t hang out that often. They run in the same circle of friends, and have a great time when they’re together, but it’s not that serious.”

Why Timothee And Kylie Seemed To Drift Apart

There have been reports that Timothee has struggled to keep up with Kylie’s fast-paced lifestyle, including the media attention that comes with it. Moreover, Timothee is reportedly concerned what the negative attention will do to his career.

The couple faced drama when attending the Golden Globes together this year, as fans are convinced there was a feud between Selena Gomez and Kylie over Timothee. This may have prompted the couple to become more private about their romance, avoiding the spotlight and public appearances, even if it fuels break-up theories.

"They’re hardly together anymore,” a source previously told OK! Magazine . “Their lives got busy, and they drifted apart. Of course, once their schedules quiet down, that could all change."

Kylie is reportedly busy focusing on her various new brands, including her Sprinter beverage and Khy fashion line, while Timothee is filming a Bob Dylan biopic in New York.
