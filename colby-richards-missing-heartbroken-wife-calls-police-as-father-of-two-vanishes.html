Colby Richards Missing Heartbroken Wife Calls Police As Father Of Two Vanishes

The heartbroken wife of a devoted father of two has taken the initiative to contact the authorities immediately after his unexplained disappearance from their home. 

Colby Richard missing case occurred when he went into the dark woods early in the morning on Friday, May 26, without closing the back gate to their property in Montgomery County, Texas.

Allison Fox, Colby’s cousin, and the family spokesperson, exclusively shared with the source that this behaviour was unusual.

This article will include the details about the Colby Richard missing case. 

The missing case 

Colby Richards is well-loved in his community for being a baseball coach and a devoted father. However, something unusual happened recently. 

He didn’t do his usual task of closing the back gate, which he always did to make sure his children and pets were safe.

This worried his wife a lot, so she quickly called the authorities for help. Allison Fox, a family member, talked about how confusing this situation is. 

Right now, there isn’t much clear information or clues to understand what happened. 

Events leading upto Colby Richards’ disappearance

Prior to his disappearance, Colby had recently secured a new position at an engineering firm, and he was highly regarded within his community for his coaching expertise with aspiring young players.

Fox News reported, on Friday, May 26, a significant day, Colby’s wife Callie observed that he left for a walk before getting ready for work. 

What made his departure unusual was that he left without his phone and keys, which he did not usually do.

Colby frequently went for walks without any electronic devices, using that time for meditation. 

However, as she explained to the source, this specific occasion felt distinct and unusual to Allison Fox.

The absence of Colby Richards 

Upon realizing that her husband was missing, Callie first checked other rooms in their house, hoping to find him resting on a couch.

However, she couldn’t find any sign of Colby inside. Worried, she went outside and discovered that the gate was wide open, which made her even more anxious. 

As a result, she immediately contacted the police, who started a thorough investigation. 

While the authorities searched the trails around their home, they only found a water bottle that belonged to Colby.

Unfortunately, this didn’t provide any significant clues about where he might be.

Allison Fox highlighted that Colby’s departure was unusual because he didn’t inform anyone about going for a walk, especially considering he had to go to work that morning. 

Callie mentioned that no notable incidents or issues in the family could explain his unexpected behavior, leaving everyone, including Callie herself, shocked and perplexed.

Investigating the Colby Richards missing case

In response to the urgent situation, multiple agencies and committed volunteers initiated a thorough search operation to find Colby. 

The community united, utilizing different methods like horses, vehicles, and all-terrain vehicles to meticulously search the county located north of Houston. 

To keep everyone informed, Allison Fox took the lead and created a dedicated Facebook page, where regular updates on the search progress were shared. 

Unfortunately, the online platform attracted false speculations and misinformation from self-proclaimed investigators, causing Fox to disable comments on the page. 

Fox strongly denied unfounded rumors that claimed Colby purposefully abandoned his family. 

Allison stressed that Colby cares deeply about his children and their well-being, and his sudden disappearance remains unexplained.

Although Colby’s athleticism and love for running make it possible that he may have gone a long way, Allison Fox is worried about him not having water for a long time. 

The search teams are working tirelessly to find him.
