Did they bleach Tom Feltons hair? Was Tom Feltons hair real in Harry Potter?

Tom Felton’s natural hair color is dark blonde. He bleached his hair platinum blonde for all eight of the Harry Potter films. Over the 10-year period the franchise was filmed, it was said that he had to color his hair every ten days.

Tom Felton said in an interview when the series had ended that he was looking forward to not having to dye his hair anymore.

Thomas Andrew Felton was born on September 22, 1987, in Epsom, United Kingdom. Tom Felton started acting in adverts for companies such as Commercial Union and Barclaycard.

Tom Felton portrayed the villain in family horror A Babysitter’s Guide to Monster Hunting (2020). Felton appeared in the sci-fi film Rise of the Planet of the Apes (2011) and the indie films From the Rough (2011) and The Apparition (2012).

Tom Felton portrayed Doctor Alchemy on The CW’s The Flash, based on the comic books of the same name. Felton was a series regular on the 2018 sci-fi series Origin and appeared as Laertes in Claire McCarthy’s Ophelia (2018).
