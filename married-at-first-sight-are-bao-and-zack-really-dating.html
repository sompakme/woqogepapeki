'Married At First Sight': Are Bao And Zack Really Dating?

Spoiler Alert: Details regarding the October 20, 2021 episode of 'Married At First Sight' are discussed below.Decision day is creeping up for the Married At First Sight couples, and it doesn't seem like too many of 'em will be sticking around for the long run. Myrla and Gil continue to defy the odds as they open up to one another, showing vulnerability, and finally taking things to a physical level.

Well, despite fans thinking Johnny and Bao would make it, it appears as if that isn't the case! Not only are fans turning against Johnny, but they seem to be doing the same to Michaela. Viewers called out Michaela following her and Zack's very first explosive fight, claiming she will be the downfall of their marriage, and perhaps fans are on to something.

Related: This Is Why Fans Are Praising Bao From 'Married At First Sight'

During tonight's episode, Michaela and Zack go at it yet again at the couple's retreat, and believe it or not, Bao stepped in. This led fans to go down a possible Zack and Bao dating rabbit hole, and it seems as if they may have found something. So, are Zack and Bao from Married At First Sight dating? Let's find out!

Michaela And Zack Are Estranged

Michaela and Zack's marriage has certainly been through the wringer since their honeymoon, leaving fans to wonder whether they'd even make it to decision day. Well, viewers got an inside look into their second go at a Michaela and Zack argument, leaving Zack with no other option than to leave the couple's retreat. Yikes!

As the couple argued at their hotel, Bao couldn't help but peek outside for a front-row seat. Not only did she get the inside scoop, but Bao actually stepped in and consoled Michaela regarding her and Zack's fight, however, it seems as if fans are clueing in on that moment meaning a lot more than producers let on.

Michaela has officially taken to Instagram to express her thoughts regarding her Married At First Sight experience, and it turns out, she and Zack are estranged! In a post to her Instagram Stories, Michaela wrote, "Look y'all! I am legally married (only because I am contractually obligated to be) but very estranged."

Michaela went on to reveal that she and Zack haven't even spoken since the show wrapped! "I have not directly spoken to that man in months," she wrote, not even referring to Zack by name. Big yikes!

Related: 'Married At First Sight': Are Myrla And Gil Defying The Odds?

Fans Think Zack And Bao Are Dating

Turns out, not only are Michaela and Zack estranged, but it appears as if Zack is allegedly dating Bao! This is a major turn of events, and with Bao having stepped in during their fight, considering the rumors going around regarding their status, it definitely adds some added fuel to the fire.

"Bao getting in the middle of Zach and Michaela is all kinds of crazy considering the rumors/confirmation," one fan tweeted out during the episode, and "crazy" is right!

Fans are also certain that producers knew that Zack and Bao were an item when they edited in Bao's involvement. One fan chimed in on Twitter saying, "They def knew Zach and Bao were dating the way they edited these scenes between the 3."

Well, Michaela is now speaking out regarding the rumors, saying that whatever Zack has going on, has nothing to do with her. In the same Instagram Stories post, Michaela wrote, "Whatever him [Zack] and her [Bao] have going on has absolutely nothing to do with me! I wish them the best," she said. Bigger yikes! Will fans watch a possible Zack and Bao romance unfold on-screen? Only time will tell.
