VSK Debuts New Look As A Trust Butler In AEW

The Trustbusters continue to expand.

At the AEW Dark tapings at Universal Studios, on August 21, VSK accompanied the group to the ring, sporting a new look. He wore a butler-like suit to the ring and came out alongside Ari Daivari, Parker Boudreaux, Slim J, and Sonny Kiss.

In a tweet, AEW president Tony Khan called him Jeeves Kay, AKA: JVS-K.

Jose the Assistant commented and hoped to differentiate his role from that of JVS-K.

@TonyKhan BUTLER, not Assistant, right? ???

— JoseTheAssistant (@JoseAssistant) August 21, 2022

The Trustbusters were featured on the August 19 episode of AEW Rampage; they lost to Orange Cassidy and the Best Friends in a first-round bout in the AEW World Trios Championship Tournament.
