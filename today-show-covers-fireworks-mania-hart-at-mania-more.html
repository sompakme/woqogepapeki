Today Show Covers Fireworks/Mania, Hart At Mania, More


– The Today Show on NBC covered the WrestleMania pyro malfunction today. They mentioned tha WWE’s pyrotechnic plans for the show were approved by the city of Orlando long before the show.

– The Washington Daily News has a tribute article to Ric Flair up WDNWeb.com.

– DH Smith worked a dark match last night prior to RAW as a heel. He also used his new nickname, the Canadian Bulldog.

– Newsday reports that a Long Island company, Zenith Pyrotechnology, handled WWE’s WrestleMania pyrotechnic display which included a malfunction that left 40 fans injured, three of whom had to be taken to a hospital. Newsday has more information on the company here.

– Former WWE star Amy “Lita” Dumas stated over the weekend that she would have agreed to be interviewed for the new Hardy Boyz DVD if asked, but wasn’t asked.

– Former WWE developmental talent Teddy Hart was at WrestleMania.
