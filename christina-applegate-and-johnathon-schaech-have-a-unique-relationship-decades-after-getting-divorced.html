Christina Applegate And Johnathon Schaech Have A Unique Relationship Decades After Getting Divorced

Highlights

  • Christina Applegate remains positive and real while discussing her Multiple Sclerosis diagnosis.
  • Applegate's first husband, Johnathon Schaech, still cares about her and supports her.
  • While the reasons behind their divorce remain unknown, Schaech has expressed that he still loves and cares about Applegate.

It must have been terrible when Christina Applegate figured out she had Multiple Sclerosis because she had to alter her lifestyle afterward. The actress has been through so much, and Applegate even suffered from another disease in the past. While no one would blame her for being angry and bitter, she has remained fairly positive while discussing her diagnosis while still keeping it real.

In countless interviews, Christina Applegate has shared that her husband Martyn LeNoble has been there for her in the wake of her health issue. Many of the Married... with Children star's fans might not know that she was married once before in the mid-2000s. Christina Applegate and her first husband Johnathon Schaech have an interesting relationship that is different than people might expect.

What Is Christina Applegate And Johnathon Schaech's Relationship Like Today?

Christina Applegate's fans were thrilled when she was one of the 2024 Emmy Awards presenters. According to Page Six, she walked with a cane that said "FU MS" and received a standing ovation from the crowd. While she said that people didn't need to clap for her, she seemed touched by the positive reception that she got.

Christina Applegate's first husband Johnathon Schaech also complimented her, which was sweet to see. He shared a picture of the actress on his Instagram Stories and wrote “We are so proud of you, Christina.”

Schaech also said:

"She danced opening night on broadway on a broken foot! Beat cancer! She’s the toughest human being I’ve ever met! F–k MS! So proud of her! Go Christina!”

While neither Applegate nor Schaech has said that they are good friends today or that they stayed close after their divorce, Schaech's comments prove that there are no hard feelings between the former couple. Many celebrities have rough and intense divorces and never speak to each other again. It's hard to imagine that most people would feel comfortable being so public about how much they care about their ex-spouse, especially because both Applegate and Schaech are famous actors. While some might want to keep their divorce entirely private, he wanted to support Applegate, which is really sweet.

According to Us Weekly, Schaech talked about Applegate in 2022 when speaking to his second wife, actress and musician Jana Kramer. He said:

“After my first marriage, I just never healed. My marriage was connected to my career, everything about my work. It was, you know, Christina is an actress. We were in the limelight. I loved her so much. I still love her so much. ... But I just never healed from Christina and I finally was starting to realize how broken my heart was."

Who Has Johnathon Schaech Been Married To?

  • Christina Applegate (2001-2007)
  • Jana Kramer (2010-2011)
  • Julie Solomon (2013-present)

It's rare for famous ex-spouses to talk about each other so positively. While Applegate doesn't seem to have talked about her divorce, at least not in recent years, Schaech has continually said that he cares about her a lot. It's also rare for someone in the public eye to be so open about their feelings. A lot of the time, stars don't want to reveal too much because they have a desire for privacy.

In 2007, Schaech was interviewed by OK Magazine and his ex-wife came up in the conversation. According to OK, they were allegedly spending time together at Les Deux, a club, and people said that they kissed. He said that they wouldn't be together again but explained, "She was the love of my life and it was great seeing her."

Both Applegate and Schaech have remarried. In fact, they both got married to their current partners in 2013. Applegate and Martyn LeNoble tied the knot that year. According to People, Applegate and LeNoble had a strong friendship that turned into something more.

Since Applegate isn't acting anymore because of MS, it was particularly moving to see her at the Emmy Awards. Her appearance proved that she is still an important part of Hollywood, even if she isn't able to star in movies or TV shows.

Why Did Christina Applegate And Johnathon Schaech Get Divorced?

Neither Christina Applegate nor Johnathon Schaech seems to have officially shared the reason why they got divorced. They haven't mentioned any big arguments, cheating incidents, or betrayals. It's possible that their marriage ran its course and they knew that they weren't meant to stay together.

Although they haven't explained why they split up, Schaech did share how he felt after the divorce. According to People, Schaech answered questions in a Reddit "Ask Me Anything" post and explained what it was like when he divorced Jana Kramer and Applegate.
