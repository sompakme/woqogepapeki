Alexi Ashe Wiki, Age (Seth Meyers Wife) Biography, Family &amp; Facts

Alexi Ashe (born April 01, 1983) is an American Attorney, Criminal Prosecutor, Assistant District Attorney, and Celebrity Partner from New Mexico. She first garnered media attention after becoming the spouse of Seth Meyers (American comedian, writer, and actor).

He also works as a producer and television host. Seth is best known for hosting a late-night talk show “Late Night with Seth Meyers” that airs on NBC. From 2001 to 2014, the actor served as a head writer and a cast member for NBC’s Saturday Night.

Meyers even hosted the show’s news parody segment, Weekend Update. Some of his other popular projects are New Year’s Eve, A.P. Bio, The Awesomes, Seth Meyers: Lobby Baby, and much more.
