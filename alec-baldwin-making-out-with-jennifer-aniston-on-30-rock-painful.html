Alec Baldwin: Making Out With Jennifer Aniston on 30 Rock &quot;Painful&quot;

Alec Baldwin is kissing and telling after getting a smooch from Jennifer Aniston on 30 Rock.

"It was painful," Baldwin joked to New York magazine at Tuesday night's Tisch on Stage gala in NYC.

Aniston, 39, played Baldwin's free-spirited stalker on the Nov. 13 episode.

"I mean, every man who's had to make out with her in TV and movies – I don't know how they do it," he added.

So does Baldwin – who has a famously contentious relationship with ex Kim Basinger – believe the show's plot that crazy women are better lovers?

"That's assuming I've been with a crazy woman," he said, wagging his finger at the New York magazine reporter. "If I answer that question in the affirmative, that would type a woman I've been with as being crazy, which I really don't feel like doing.

"But I hear it's true – I hear from my friends it's true," he added.

Aniston's appearance earned the show its lowest ratings of the season with only 7.5 million viewers. Oprah's Nov. 6 appearance drew 8 million.
