The Real Housewives of Miami News

Network:Bravo

The seventh installment of The Real Housewives franchise, The Real Housewives of Miami (RHOM) follows the personal and professional drama of several women living in Miami. The show initially aired on Bravo from 2011 to 2013 before being revived by Peacock in 2021 for seasons 4 and 5. In May 2023, Bravo announced that RHOM would return to the network for its sixth season, while also continuing to stream on Peacock.

Notable cast members of the show through the years include Guerdy Abraira, Lisa Hochstein, Larsa Pippen, Adriana De Moura, Julia Lemigova, Nicole Martin, Alex Nepola, Marysol Patton and Kiki Barth.
