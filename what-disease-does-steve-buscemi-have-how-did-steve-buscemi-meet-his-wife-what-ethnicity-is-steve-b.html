Steven Vincent Buscemi was born on December 13, 1957, in New York City.  He is American, however, he has Italian, Dutch, Irish and English ancestry from his parents.

Buscemi is famous for his roles in Quentin Tarantino’s Reservoir Dogs, Simon West’s Con Air, Ghost World, The Death of Stalin, Michael Bay’s Armageddon, Big Fish, and  Rodriguez’s Desperado.

Buscemi also collaborated with the Coen brothers in some films including; Miller’s Crossing, The Big Lebowski, Barton Fink, The Hudsucker Proxy, Fargo and Paris, Je t’aime.

In 1987, Buscemi married Jo Andres. The pair were married till her death in 2019. They have a son named Lucian.
