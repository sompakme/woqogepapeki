Early Life and Education of David Oyelowo

David Oyelowo, a renowned British actor, was born on April 1, 1976, in Oxford, England. His early life was shaped by his experiences in both the UK and Nigeria before eventually returning to London. Oyelowo’s passion for the arts led him to study theater at the prestigious London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art (LAMDA).

During his time at LAMDA, Oyelowo honed his skills and embarked on his acting journey. He began his career on stage, captivating audiences with his performances. As his talent grew, he ventured into television and film, making his mark in the entertainment industry.

As of today, David Oyelowo is 47 years old and stands at a height of 5 ft 9 in (1.778 m). His early life and education have provided a solid foundation for his successful career, allowing him to explore his craft and captivate audiences around the world.
