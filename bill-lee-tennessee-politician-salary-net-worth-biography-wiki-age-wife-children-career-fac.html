William Byron Lee (born October 9, 1959) is a well-known American businessman and politician, working as the fiftieth and last governor of Tennessee. Elected in 2018, Lee ran as a business-oriented member of the Republican Party. Before entering politics, he held numerous jobs at the Lee Corporation, an inherited family firm; he was the company’s president and CEO from 1992 to 2016.
