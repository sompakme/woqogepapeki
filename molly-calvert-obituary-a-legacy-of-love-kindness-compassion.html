Molly Calvert Obituary: A Legacy of Love, Kindness, &amp; Compassion

Molly Calvert obituary serves as a tribute to a life lived with compassion, kindness, and an unwavering dedication to others.

A vibrant and compassionate soul, Molly Calvert, left us all too soon at the age of 24. Born on September 12, 1998, in Pittsburgh, Molly was the beloved daughter of Barry and Meg (Sirera) Calvert. 

The news of Molly Calvert’s death on Monday, May 8, 2023, weighs heavily on the hearts of those who knew her.

Her graduation from Gannon University in 2021 with a Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN) was a testament to her hard work, resilience, and her deep-rooted passion for helping others. It was clear to all who knew her that Molly’s purpose in life was to serve and care for those in need.

Before embarking on her nursing career, Molly Calvert dedicated her time as a lifeguard and swim teacher from 2015 to 2019. It was during this period that she shared her passion for water safety, nurturing the skills of those she taught and ensuring their well-being.

After achieving her BSN, Molly joined the compassionate team at UPMC Hamot as a Nurse, where she continued to make a positive impact on the lives of patients and their families. Her commitment to the nursing profession was exemplary and recognized by her involvement in various organizations.

Molly’s contributions extended beyond her professional achievements. She actively served as an Executive Board Member of the Alpha Sigma Alpha Gannon Chapter, demonstrating her leadership qualities. 

Additionally, she was a valued member of the Honors Society of Nursing, Common Bonds, and Sigma Theta Tau, highlighting her commitment to personal growth and the pursuit of knowledge.

While Molly’s professional accomplishments were commendable, it was her deep love for her family and friends that truly defined her character. 

Her loved ones meant everything to her, and she embraced them wholeheartedly. Molly’s loyalty and selflessness were unmatched, always going above and beyond to support those she cared about.

As we reflect on Molly Calvert’s life, we celebrate the cherished memories she created and the profound impact she had on her family, friends, and community. 

Her legacy of kindness, dedication, and unwavering support will forever remain in the hearts of those who were fortunate enough to have known her.

But above all, spending time with her family brought her the greatest joy. They shared countless cherished moments, creating memories that will forever hold a special place in their hearts.

Molly Calvert’s visitation is scheduled for Sunday, May 14th, 2023, and invites her family and friends to attend.

The visitation hours are scheduled from 1-3 PM and 5-7 PM at the Spencer D. Geibel Funeral Home & Cremation Services, located at 140 New Castle Rd, Butler, PA 16001. 

After the visitation, the funeral home will host a service, bringing everyone together to commemorate Molly’s life in a meaningful way.

Molly Calvert obituary is a testament to a life well-lived, a life dedicated to making a positive difference in the world.

As we honor Molly’s memory, let us also strive to embody the same qualities that made her so special—loyalty, compassion, and a willingness to help others.
