Newhart Husband

Ginny Newhart first met her future husband, Bob Newhart, on a blind date, set up by comedian Buddy Hackett, in Los Angeles in 1962. She was serving as the babysitter for Hackett’s children at the time when one day he told her that he had met a young comic actor, adding that “he’s Catholic and you’re Catholic, and I think maybe you should marry each other”.

However, Ginny did not want to marry someone from the showbiz like her father who would not get a paycheck every week. Nevertheless, they soon met, had dinner together and played pool at Hackett’s house. They eventually married on January 12, 1963. She was unsure even on the day of her marriage when her father reportedly had joked that he could still get her out of it if she wanted.Fortunately, she never had to regret marrying Bob, and after spending five decades together with him, she told ‘City and Shore Magazine’ in an interview in January 2013 that “it turned out fine”.
