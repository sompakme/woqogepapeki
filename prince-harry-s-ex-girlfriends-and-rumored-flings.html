Prince Harrys Ex-Girlfriends and Rumored Flings

Before Prince Harry decided to settle down and marry Meghan Markle, his relationship status often made headlines. From his rumored hookup with a former Real Housewife to his on-again, off-again relationship with Chelsy Davy, Us Weekly breaks down his dating history.

“For so many people in the family, especially obviously the men, there can be a temptation or an urge to marry someone who fits the mold,” the prince explained in the first episode of Netflix’s Harry & Meghan docuseries, which debuted in December 2022. “As opposed to someone who perhaps you may be destined to be with. [It’s] the difference between making decisions with your head or your heart.”

The Duke of Sussex noted that all of his past girlfriends had their lives “turned upside down” when they started dating the famous royal.

“[I learned] the pain and suffering of women marrying into this institution. I remember thinking, ‘How can I ever find someone who’s willing and capable to be able to withstand all the baggage that comes with being with me?’” he said.

Harry met Meghan during summer 2016 after he asked a friend to introduce them when he saw the Suits star on social media.

“Meghan and I met over Instagram. I was scrolling through my feed, and someone who was a friend had this video of the two of them, it was like a Snapchat,” he recalled.

Meghan revealed she was using the puppy filter in the footage. “That was the first thing, I was like, ‘Who is that?’” Harry added.

He proposed in fall 2017 and they wed the following May.

“I remember my family first meeting her and being incredibly impressed,” Harry said on the series. “Some of them didn’t quite know what to do with themselves, so I think they were surprised, maybe surprised that the ginger could land such a beautiful woman and such an intelligent woman. But the fact that I was dating an American actress was probably what clouded their judgment more than anything else at the beginning: ‘Oh, she’s an American actress. This won’t last.'”
