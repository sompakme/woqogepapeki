WWE Crown Jewel: Bianca Belair vs. Bayley Result

continues to prove why she’s “The EST,” taking down Bayley in an incredible Last Woman Standing Match at WWE Crown Jewel.

In a match that lived up to the hype between both superstars, it was Belair who would come out on top to once again keep a hold of WWE RAW Women’s Championship. The match saw both superstars fighting all over the arena, including Bayley even attempting to lock Belair in a chest and utilize a golf cart to try and come out on top.

Unfortunately for her, it wouldn’t matter, as Belair eventually won after trapping Bayley in between a ladder that was pinned in between the lower rungs of one of the turnbuckles.

For a recap of how the match ended, check out below courtesy of our live, play-by-play coverage:

The referee counts. Bayley gets to her feet. Belair grabs the kendo stick as Bayley runs away. Belair whips Bayley with the kendo stick. Bayley hits the Bayley-to-Belly on the stage. The referee counts. Both women get to their feet at the eight count. Bayley hits Belair with an equipment case. Bayley traps Belair in the case. Belair bursts out of the case as the referee is counting. Belair tries the KOD but Bayley escapes. Bayley locks Belair in the crossface. Belair passes out. The referee starts his count. Belair gets to her feet and falls right back down. Bayley drives out on the stage in a golf cart. Bayley tries to run over Belair, but she moves out of the way.

Bayley climbs on the top of the cart. Belair follows and slams Bayley’s head into the cart. Belair drives Bayley back down to the ring. Belair tosses Bayley off the top of the cart onto a table that doesn’t break. Bayley isn’t moving. Belair picks up Bayley and powerbombs her through the table. The referee counts. Bayley breaks the count at nine. Belair lays Bayley on a chair and puts another one on her chest. Belair tries a 450 splash, but Bayley moves out of the way.

The referee counts as Belair screams in agony. Belair gets to her feet at the 9.5 count. Bayley sets up a chair. Belair lifts Bayley and hits a KOD on the chair. Bayley’s knee hits the point of the chair. that was a nasty landing. Belair traps Bayley in between a ladder and slides it under the bottom rope. Bayley is trapped. Bayley only makes it halfway out before the referee counts to 10.
