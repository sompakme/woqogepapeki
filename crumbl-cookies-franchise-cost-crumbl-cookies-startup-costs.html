How much does a Crumbl Cookies franchise cost?

Investing in a Crumbl Cookies franchise can be an exciting opportunity, but it’s important to understand the associated costs. The cost of starting a Crumbl Cookies franchise can vary depending on factors such as location and size. On average, the initial investment for a Crumbl Cookies franchise ranges from $347,666 to $691,783.

This initial investment includes various expenses, such as leasehold improvements, equipment, inventory, and working capital. These costs are necessary to set up and operate a successful Crumbl Cookies franchise. Additionally, franchisees are required to have at least $150,000 in liquid capital, which ensures they have sufficient funds to support the business during the initial stages.

It’s important to note that these figures are averages, and the actual cost may vary depending on specific circumstances and market conditions. However, the investment in a Crumbl Cookies franchise provides access to a proven business model, a recognizable brand, and ongoing support from the corporate team.

As with any investment, it’s crucial to thoroughly evaluate the potential returns and conduct due diligence before making a decision. Consulting with financial advisors and franchise experts can provide valuable insights into the financial viability of a Crumbl Cookies franchise and help determine if it aligns with your goals and resources.

How much does a Crumbl Cookies franchise owner make?

The profitability of a Crumbl Cookies franchise can vary depending on factors such as location, market demand, and management. According to the financial data from 115 Crumbl franchise owners in 2021, the highest revenue generated by a store was $3,639,139, while the lowest revenue was $734,278. The average revenue for all stores was $1,687,731. The highest net profit for a store was $618,102, while the lowest net profit was $33,260. The average net profit for all locations was $357,512.
