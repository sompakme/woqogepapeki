Early Life and Career Beginnings

Alli Webb’s journey to becoming a successful entrepreneur began early in life. Growing up in South Florida, Webb had an innate passion for beauty and fashion. The Creation of Drybar

Alli Webb’s passion for making women feel confident and beautiful led her to create Drybar, a salon that offers high-quality blowouts at an affordable price. Before opening Drybar’s first location in Brentwood, California, in 2010, Alli Webb worked as a professional hair stylist in Los Angeles.

During her time working in hair salons, Alli noticed a gap in the market. Many women wanted a quick and easy blowout without the expensive and time-consuming salon experience. She saw an opportunity to offer a specialized service that focused solely on blowouts, and thus Drybar was born.

Alli’s vision for Drybar quickly became a reality thanks to her tireless effort and entrepreneurial spirit. She designed the salon to look and feel like a beauty bar, with a menu of different hairstyles named after her favorite drinks. The concept was an instant success, and Drybar became a go-to destination for women seeking a quick and affordable hair makeover.

Despite initial skepticism from some investors and stylists, Alli’s unwavering dedication to her vision paid off. Drybar became a household name and expanded rapidly, with over 100 locations across the US and Canada. Alli’s success with Drybar paved the way for other business ventures, including co-founding Squeeze, a massage concept.

Alli Webb’s success story is a testament to her entrepreneurial vision and her ability to revolutionize an industry. Drybar’s creation changed the blowout game, providing women with a convenient and affordable way to look and feel their best.
